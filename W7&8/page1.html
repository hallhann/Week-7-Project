My top 3 favorite drinks:

  1. Coffee
  2. Boba
  3. Redbull
Coffee definitely stands at my #1 option. My go-to is a white chocolate mocha. Coffee is usually my study drink.

Boba tea is a close second to coffee. My favorite shop where I live is called Societea. The staff always knows my order: Jasmine milk tea with soy milk. Boba is usually my pick-me-up drink after a hard day.

Last on the list is Redbull. This is last on the list because it's definitely not the healthiest option. My favorite flavor is coconut. I usually get a Redbull if I know I'm going to have a long day at work or if I need to stay up late to work on school.
